Master's Thesis

  • Title: A Study on the Reconceptualization of Sharing and a Case Analysis of the Sharing Economy ( : )
  • Advisor: Dr. Tae Young Kim
  • Year: 2018
  • Sharing has become a rhetorical device in marketing and public policy, as it carries connotations of community. The concept of sharing generates confusion in public policy because it has multiple and ambiguous meanings. This study explores the concept of sharing, develops an ideal type of sharing, and analyzes the case of car-sharing in Seoul using this framework.
